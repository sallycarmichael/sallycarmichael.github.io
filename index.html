Abraham Lincoln once said: “The problem with quotes on the internet is that you can’t be sure of their accuracy.”

He raises an important point. With the age of the internet and countless quotation websites, we’ve come to realize just how ubiquitous misquotations are.

This site serves as a compilation of popular misquotations as a token of respect to the people who never said those things. It’s not nice to have words involuntarily shoved down your throat.
